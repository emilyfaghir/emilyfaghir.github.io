About Me

Hi, my name is Emily Faghir, and welcome to my digital portfolio! This portfolio is by no means professional. It serves as a place to store my memories. I started taking photos as a way to capture these moments and be able to relive them. All of these photos you see were taken for an individual reason; behind them was an intense emotion I was feeling that led me to take the photo, whether it was documenting a picture of a friend, a cultural experience I had while traveling, or something stunning.

More about me! I am a Junior at Northeastern University, studying Business Analytics and Economics.
